The Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) is a global Pentecostal megachurch founded in 1952 in Lagos, Nigeria, by Rev. Josiah Olufemi Akindayomi. Under the leadership of Pastor Enoch Adejare Adeboye, it has grown exponentially, with thousands of parishes in over 190 countries. RCCG emphasizes holiness, evangelism, and the belief in the power of prayer. It is known for its annual Convention and Holy Ghost Congress, which attracts millions of worshippers worldwide. The church's mission is to spread the gospel of Jesus Christ and prepare the world for His Second Coming.
